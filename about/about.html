About The Techerz


At The Techerz, we believe in unlocking the potential of every student through the power of technology and creativity. Founded with a vision to inspire the innovators of tomorrow, we’ve spent the last five years creating a vibrant learning environment where young minds explore, experiment, and excel.

Our diverse range of programs—including learning, animations, game and app development, artificial intelligence, robotics, and microcontroller projects—offers something for every curious mind. With a global community of 100+ students across 5 countries, we’ve built a reputation for making learning both engaging and impactful.

More than just coding, we focus on fostering critical thinking, problem-solving, and teamwork, ensuring that every student is not just a learner but a creator.

Why Choose The Techerz?


🧑‍🏫 Experienced Mentors, Personalized Learning: With over 5+ years of expertise in STEM and robotics education, our mentors offer personalized guidance tailored to each student's learning journey.

🛠 Hands-On, Real-World Learning: Through project-based activities, students gain practical experience that strengthens their confidence, creativity, and critical thinking.

🚀 Access to Cutting-Edge Technology: From Arduino to 3D printing and more, students work with the latest tools to stay ahead in a fast-evolving tech world.

🌍 Global Reach with a Local Touch: With a vibrant community across 5 countries, we blend global exposure with a personal, welcoming learning environment.

🎯 A Future-Ready Approach: Whether mastering tech skills or participating in international competitions like FLL, WRO, and F1, students are prepared to dream big and lead confidently.

👩‍💻 Programs for All Learning Levels: We offer sessions for ages 4 to 18, extend programs to college students, and provide professional training for teachers, making technology education accessible at every stage.

✨ An Inspiring Space to Learn and Grow: At Coders Studio, learning is designed to be engaging, exciting, and full of discovery, keeping curiosity alive in every session.

At The Techerz, we’re more than just educators—we’re mentors, innovators, and cheerleaders for every step of your child’s journey. Together, let’s turn ideas into reality and dreams into achievements!
